Frequently Asked Questions

What are the key components of SEO?

Key components of SEO include keyword research, on-page optimization, quality content, technical SEO, backlinks, user experience, and analytics monitoring.

How does technical SEO affect my website?

Technical SEO helps in improving the ability of search engine crawlers to access your site, indexing of your site, site speed, and security, thus leading to improved search rankings and better user experience..

How often should I update my website for SEO?

Frequent updates should occur within a few-week interval in order to ensure optimization, update the content by making it more user friendly and addressing the technical issues.

Can I do SEO on my own, or do I need an expert?

Yes, SEO can be managed independently, but many a times the expert knowledge and experience will provide more efficient approaches to the problem and provide faster results.
